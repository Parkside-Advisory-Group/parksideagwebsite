Frequently Asked

Questions buyers ask before they book.

What does Parkside Advisory Group do?

Parkside builds practical AI systems for service businesses. The systems answer every inbound lead within 60 seconds, follow up automatically over SMS, book appointments, and recover revenue from missed calls and stale estimates. We install, we train, we stay on for monthly tuning.

How much does a Parkside AI system cost?

Every engagement is custom scoped based on your specific bottlenecks, lead volume, and operational complexity.

Because we don't sell one-size-fits-all products, every engagement begins with a free 30-minute diagnostic assessment. This allows us to identify your exact revenue leaks and recommend which specific AI capabilities from our stable of tools you need to plug them.

How long does installation take?

Two to three weeks from signed agreement to a live system. That includes integration with your phone, CRM, and calendar, plus training on the reporting dashboard. Tier 03 (Revenue Recovery Suite) can extend to four weeks when we're wiring up multiple campaigns.

Do I need to switch CRMs or phone systems?

No. Parkside integrates with the tools you already use — ServiceTitan, Jobber, Housecall Pro, HubSpot, Google Sheets, or a plain business phone line. If you don't have a CRM yet, we can spin up a simple one as part of the install. We're not here to sell you new software.

What industries do you focus on?

We work with service businesses that depend on fast lead response, booked appointments, and consistent follow-up. The underlying system — answer the lead, follow up, book the job, recover stale opportunities — generalizes well across appointment-driven operators.

What if I'm not technical?

You don't need to be. Parkside handles the full build, integration, and training. What you'll see is a simple dashboard, a one-page monthly report, and a direct line to the operator who installed your system. If something needs to change, you send a text or an email — not a support ticket.

How fast will I see results?

Lead response time drops to under 60 seconds the day the system goes live. Most businesses see measurable increases in booked appointments within the first 30 days, and meaningful revenue recovery from re-engaged leads and follow-up campaigns within 60 to 90 days. We track it in the monthly report and tune from there.

Where is Parkside based, and do you work remotely?

Parkside is based in Charlotte, North Carolina. We work with service businesses across North Carolina, South Carolina, and the broader United States. Every engagement runs remote-first — the entire install process is designed so you never have to take a tech off a job site for a meeting.
