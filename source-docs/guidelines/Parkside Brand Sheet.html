01   Design decisions

What changed and why

Wordmark

Dropped the heavy block sans. Set in Newsreader, a contemporary serif with low stress and wide counters — the same family of voice used by firms like BCG or Bridgewater. Sentence case follows the convention at our reference bar — McKinsey & Company, Bain & Company, Evercore, Lazard — confident without being monumental. Tracking is set to -0.012em so letters sit together as a shape, not a row.

Tagline

“Advisory Group” is now set in Inter Tight Medium at 0.22em tracking — open enough to breathe, tight enough to hold at 14px in an email signature. The previous 0.3em read as stretched; at the new value the word spaces are optically balanced against the letter spaces.

Mark & divider

The three-bar growth symbol stays. The vertical gold divider is removed — it was a fifth element that confused favicon renders. The gold baseline is kept as a thin 3-unit rule, which anchors the bars and carries a whisper of the original identity.

03   The mark

Three bars. One baseline.

Construction

Bars share a common 6° slant on their top edge, rising left-to-right. Heights progress 28u, 38u, 56u — a near-golden progression that reads as growth without being literal. Gold baseline sits 6u below bar bottoms, width 82u, height 3u.

Behaviour at scale

At and below 24px, the baseline disappears optically and the three bars hold the identity. The mark is built on a 120-unit grid; all strokes and gaps are integers of that unit so the shape stays crisp in raster exports.

Usage ratio

Cream and ink carry the page. Burgundy is reserved for the mark, primary buttons, and moments of emphasis — roughly 5% of any composition. Gold is a supporting accent used for rules, dividers, and callouts — never larger than the burgundy it accompanies. Avoid tints; use the colours at full strength.

06   Use

Clear space & what to avoid

Clear space · minimum "P"
Parkside
Advisory Group

Maintain clear space equal to the x-height of the wordmark (the height of a lowercase “p”) on all sides of the lockup. Do not place type, rules, or imagery inside that margin.

Minimum size
Parkside
Advisory Group
120 px wide · absolute minimum

The horizontal lockup must not be reproduced below 120 px wide on screen or 30 mm in print. Below that, use the mark alone.
