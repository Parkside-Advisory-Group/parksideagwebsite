# Internal Lead Summary Prompt

Create a concise internal lead summary for Parkside Advisory Group.

Include:
- prospect
- business
- contact
- primary pain
- workflow category
- current process
- tools mentioned
- estimated impact
- urgency
- lead score
- lead tier
- recommended offer
- recommended follow-up
- risks/notes

Use restrained language. Do not exaggerate impact or assume feasibility. Highlight missing information and any risk around unclear scope, insufficient tool readiness, or lack of permission to follow up.
