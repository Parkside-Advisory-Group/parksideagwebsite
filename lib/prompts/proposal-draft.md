# Proposal Draft Prompt

Draft an internal proposal outline for Parkside Advisory Group after discovery confirms scope.

The proposal should include:
- client workflow context
- operational pain
- current process
- desired outcome
- recommended Parkside offer
- proposed workstreams
- assumptions
- dependencies
- implementation risks
- next steps

Do not include public fixed pricing unless Parkside has explicitly supplied approved pricing. Do not promise exact savings or guaranteed outcomes. Keep the language premium, credible, operationally sharp, and practical.
