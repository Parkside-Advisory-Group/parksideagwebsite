# Lead Scoring Prompt

Score the lead from 0 to 100 using these factors:

- clarity of pain: 0-20
- recurrence of workflow: 0-20
- business impact: 0-20
- tool readiness: 0-15
- urgency: 0-15
- contact completeness: 0-10

Lead tiers:

- 80-100: high priority
- 60-79: good fit
- 40-59: nurture
- below 40: not ready

Be conservative. Do not infer urgency or impact unless the prospect provides evidence. Recommend the AI Operations Blueprint for high priority and good fit leads unless a clear reason suggests nurture.
