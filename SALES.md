# Sales

## Primary CTA

Start an AI Operations Blueprint.

## Qualification Signals

Strong-fit prospects usually have:

- a recurring workflow pain
- clear business impact
- manual follow-up or visibility gaps
- existing tools that can be integrated or used as source systems
- a near-term desire to improve operations
- permission to follow up

## Lead Tiers

- 80-100: high priority
- 60-79: good fit
- 40-59: nurture
- below 40: not ready

## Recommended Follow-Up

High priority and good fit leads should be invited to an AI Operations Blueprint discussion. Nurture leads should receive a practical request for more context. Not-ready leads should receive a light educational follow-up without pressure.

Do not discuss final pricing before discovery.
