## Position

Parkside Advisory Group should feel premium, credible, operationally sharp, high-touch, calm, strategic, trustworthy, and plainspoken.

## Visual Identity

- Primary burgundy: `#7A1F2B`
- Gold: `#B08A3E`
- Gold light: `#C9A765`
- Ink: `#1A1613`
- Ink soft: `#3A3430`
- Cream: `#F6F1E8`
- Cream deep: `#EEE7D7`
- Dark: `#141210`

## Typography

- Primary headings and wordmark style: Newsreader, weight 500.
- UI, labels, navigation, and CTAs: Inter Tight, weights 500-700.
- Metadata and system labels: JetBrains Mono, weights 400-500.

## Assets

Use SVG logos and marks from `source-docs/`. The Next.js app copies selected assets into `public/brand/`.

## Voice

Use:

- reduce manual work
- improve follow-up
- create operational visibility
- streamline recurring workflows
- practical AI systems
- AI-supported operating systems
- workflow automation
- client intake
- reporting visibility
- business process improvement

Avoid:

- revolutionize
- unlock potential
- cutting-edge AI
- AI transformation
- guaranteed savings
- fully autonomous everything
- generic agency language
